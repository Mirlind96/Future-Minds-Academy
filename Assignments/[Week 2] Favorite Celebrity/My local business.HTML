KFC

KFC is an international fast-food restaurant chain specializing in preparing fried chicken. It is an iconic fast-food brand and one of the most recognized restaurants in the world.

KFC's menu offers a wide range of chicken-based products, including its famous fried chicken and other delicious items.

KFC was founded by Colonel Sanders in 1930 and has been successful worldwide ever since. The restaurant provides fast service, tasty food, and a unique experience for customers who love fried chicken.
